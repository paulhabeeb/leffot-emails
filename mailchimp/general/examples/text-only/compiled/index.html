We’re Open!
We are excited to announce that our New York City boutique has reopened. In order to welcome you safely, we would like to share some new guidelines to give you peace of mind when you visit us.
Personal Protect­ive Equip­ment
For your safety and the safety of others, masks are required at all times.
Social Distan­cing
The number of people in the inside will be managed to ensure that every­one can remain six feet apart.
Hygiene and Clean­ing
We will be disin­fect­ing all high-touch surfaces and have hand santit­izers ready for your use.
Priv­ate Appoint­ments
We are happy to accom­mod­ate special times for you if you would prefer. Please email us at info@leffot.com to sched­ule an appoint­ment earlier or after hours.
New Store Hours
For the time being, we will be open Monday to Saturday from 12 pm to 6 pm.
We look forward to seeing you soon!
