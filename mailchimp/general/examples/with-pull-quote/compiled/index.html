Customize a Pair of Corthays
Dive into our selection of custom made Corthay shoes.
Last year we launched a new website, and with it the ability to customize Edward Green and Gaziano & Girling shoes. We recently expanded this feature to include Corthay.
Corthay shoes are sleek, modern, and quintessentially French. With elegance and sophistication, they are small works of art.
“A pair of sculpted-toe Casanova oxfords in lie de vin calfskin may be just the thing to celebrate in.”
Read Robb Report’s feature on our collection of Corthays available for custom order.
Take your pick from a number of models, including their iconic two-eyelet derby, the Arca; devilishly handsome oxford, the Casanova; sleek ankle-height chukka, the Bella; and many more. Each model can be configured to your liking, from the leather and patina to the last and sole.
Use it to place an order today, or simply find inspiration for the future.
